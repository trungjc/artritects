About

This theme is integrated with AddtoAny Service, people can signal your articles to social bookmarking services, bookmark in IE o Firefox or send Articles to Email. We reccomend to use WordPress Plugin: Challenge together with WP Hashcash Plugin for Spam, they're all 2.7 compatible.
